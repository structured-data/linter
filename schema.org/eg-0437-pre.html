Name: The Falles 2017 Description: The Falles is a traditional celebration held in commemoration of Saint Joseph in the city of Valencia, Spain. The term Falles refers to both the celebration and the monuments burnt during the celebration. Added to UNESCO's intangible cultural heritage of humanity list on 30 November 2016.
